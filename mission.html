Our Mission

Your data. Your infrastructure.
Your alpha.

← Back to the stack

Most agent infrastructure assumes a platform, a subscription, and your trust. We build the opposite: components you install, own, and can read line by line — memory that lives in your files, security that answers to you, and nothing that phones home.

Every module in this stack exists because we personally hit a wall the public tools couldn't handle — and built the fix we wished existed. We run everything we ship, on our own agents, every day. Nothing goes public that we don't use ourselves.

We're an independent studio giving solo builders a fighting chance: the solopreneurs, first-time founders, and grant-stage teams working to reverse their position in a K-shaped economy. The infrastructure sovereignty a corporation buys with a platform team — delivered as components you pip install. When the platforms pivot, your stack doesn't. That's the thesis of The Karp Moment →

Solo isn't a company size — it's a role. Whether you're self-employed or the only developer shipping a project inside a thousand-person org, this is for you. And if you're one person wearing five hats, a team of agents is how you wear all five.

Solo builders first — but not solo builders only. As the stack matures, we take on enterprise engagements: custom agents, integrations, infrastructure built to your constraints, on terms negotiated openly. If your team wants this built for you, talk to us →

The core stack is open source and free, forever — and safety fixes are never gated. Complex capital-building tools will be paid products, on terms stated plainly. No hidden meters. Ever.
